About Biff Lewis Geomatics

30+ years of professional land surveying in Cape Town and the Western Cape — built on precision, ethics, and a can-do approach.

Our Story

A Practice Built on Listening

Biff Lewis Geomatics was established with a clear philosophy: professional surveying begins with listening. We take time to understand each client's specific needs, define the problem correctly, and then apply the right expertise and tools to deliver results that stand up to scrutiny.

Based in Observatory, Cape Town, we have grown into one of the Western Cape's leading land survey practices — serving a diverse range of clients from individual homeowners to municipalities, developers, engineers, and attorneys.

Our approach has remained consistent throughout: maintain the highest ethical standards, invest in up-to-date equipment and software, develop our team through mentorship, and never compromise on accuracy. Every deliverable we issue carries our professional certification — and the responsibility that comes with it.

We welcome complex challenges. If a project seems difficult, our answer is still "can do" — and we back that confidence with the expertise and tools to deliver.

Our Values

What Drives Everything We Do

Our professional values shape every project we take on — from the first conversation to the final certified report.

Precision First

Accurate measurements are non-negotiable. We calibrate our equipment, verify our data, and never cut corners — because the consequences of inaccuracy in land surveying are real and lasting.

Ethical Conduct

We operate with full transparency and integrity. Our professional registrations require it — and our clients deserve it. Every report we produce reflects both our expertise and our professional integrity.

Prompt Response

Construction timelines don't wait. We understand the urgency of our clients' projects and consistently deliver fast turnaround times without compromising on quality.

Client-Centred

We listen before we act. Understanding a client's actual needs — not just the technical brief — leads to better proposals, better solutions, and better outcomes.

Modern Methods

We invest continuously in the latest survey equipment and software — total stations, GPS/GNSS, CAD, and GIS tools — so our results always reflect current professional standards.

Staff Development

Our team is our greatest asset. We invest in mentorship and ongoing training to ensure every member of the Biff Lewis Geomatics team performs to the highest professional standard.

Credentials & Registration

Professional Standards You Can Rely On

Our registrations, certifications, and working practices meet the highest standards demanded by South African geomatics legislation and professional bodies.

  • South African Geomatics Council (SAGC) Registered

    Registered with the statutory body governing professional land surveyors in South Africa — a mandatory requirement for issuing legally recognised survey documents.

  • Professional Land Surveyor Certification

    All survey work is executed and certified by a registered professional surveyor — giving clients, attorneys, and municipalities confidence in every deliverable.

  • Calibrated Survey Equipment

    We use calibrated total stations, GPS/GNSS receivers, and survey-grade instruments — maintained and verified regularly to ensure measurement accuracy.

  • Current CAD & GIS Software

    All technical drawings, models, and mapping outputs are produced using industry-standard software to the latest specification requirements.

  • 30+ Years of Western Cape Experience

    Decades of operating across Cape Town and the broader Western Cape region has given us deep knowledge of local land tenure, municipal systems, and terrain challenges.

  • Ongoing Training & Mentorship

    We continuously invest in staff development, ensuring every team member is current with professional standards, legislation, and best practices.

How We Work

From First Call to Final Report

A clear, structured process so you always know where your project stands.

  1. 1

    Briefing & Proposal

    We listen carefully to your needs, define the scope of work, and provide a clear written proposal with a fee estimate.

  2. 2

    Field Survey

    Our team visits the site with calibrated equipment to collect all required measurements and spatial data.

    You receive a professionally certified, signed deliverable ready for use in planning, legal, or construction processes.
